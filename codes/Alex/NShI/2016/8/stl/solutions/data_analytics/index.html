From customer behavior and supply chains, to financial and employee conditions, to social media and security threats, business and government agencies produce and capture ever-increasing amounts of data. Unlocking value within these data assets is the purpose of analytics. SGI High Performance Solutions for Data Analytics help you unlock value at the speed of memory, with near limitless scale and mission-critical reliability using SAP HANA®, Oracle® Database In-Memory, and Apache® Hadoop™.

SGI's leadership in data analytics derives from deep expertise in High Performance Computing (HPC) and over two decades delivering many of the world's fastest supercomputers. Leveraging this experience and SGI's innovative shared and distributed memory computing solutions for data analytics enables organizations to achieve greater insight, accelerate innovation, and gain competitive advantage.

High Performance Solutions for Data Analytics
SGI UV 300H is a model of the SGI UV 300 supercomputer designed for large or growing SAP HANA environments and enables running critical applications on SAP HANA including SAP Business Suite, SAP Bank Analyzer, SAP S/4HANA Finance, and other SAP S/4HANA applications. Currently SAP-certified from 4 to 20 sockets and 20TBs of computing capacity, the future-ready UV 300H is designed to scale seamlessly to 32 sockets and 32TB of cache-coherent shared memory as a single node.

A new model of the SGI UV 300 supercomputer designed for large or growing Oracle environments, SGI UV 300RL enables enterprises to run Oracle Database In-Memory on a single, Intel-based system with un-paralleled scale-up capacity. Certified with Oracle Linux 7 with Unbreakable Enterprise Kernel, UV 300RL scales from 4 to 32 sockets and 1 to 24TBs of cache-coherent shared memory to accelerate data analytics and achieve real-time operations.

To tackle highly complex data relationships using graph databases, or to achieve time to value in seconds with other powerful analytic engines such as the Oracle TimesTen relational database, the broader SGI® UV™ line encompasses the world's most powerful in-memory computing systems, delivering unsurpassed scalability, single-system simplicity, and widely proven SGI technology.

For batch analysis and extending volumes of unstructured and historical data, an HPC cluster with the Apache Hadoop framework is ideal - and for which SGI has been at the forefront with tens of thousands of Hadoop nodes in operation. SGI® Rackable® servers provide high value compute for small to large enterprise Hadoop clusters. For extreme-scale and optimized for Hadoop, SGI® InfiniteData™ Cluster delivers two or more times the compute and storage capacity per rack of any competing offering.

SGI Professional Services and 3rd party experts will work closely with business lines and your IT organization to deliver a complete solution, designed to your needs and coupled with SGI's world-class support. For High Performance Solutions for Data Analytics, look to SGI.
