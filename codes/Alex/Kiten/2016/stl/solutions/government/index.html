Government & SGI Federal

A Long History of Working with Leading Security, Defense and Intelligence Communities

SGI has a long history of providing compute and archiving solutions to our nation's leading security, defense and intelligence communities. Our focus is to provide leading-edge information technology solutions to our U.S. Federal government customers by partnering with federal system integrators and application providers. We offer dedicated sales, support, professional services, and program staff, including cleared personnel, to better understand and develop the most optimal solutions for your requirements.

SGI Federal

SGI Federal is the wholly owned subsidiary of SGI that provides products and services to our U.S. Federal government customers.
