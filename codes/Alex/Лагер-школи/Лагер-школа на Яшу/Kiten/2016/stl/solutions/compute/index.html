From government and intelligence organizations, to leading universities and advanced research institutions, to businesses on the cutting-edge, SGI High Performance Solutions for Compute are hard at work. SGI provides the fastest distributed-memory and largest in-memory supercomputers in the world, which running Linux coupled with innovative SGI software for system and power management, deliver an unprecedented combination of speed, scale, and efficiency. Earning numerous industry awards for high performance computing (HPC), SGI scale-up and scale-out computing solutions help customers find answers to their biggest challenges.

Scale Up Solutions

SGI® UV™ in-memory computing systems are the most powerful in the world. Delivering up to 64TB of cache-coherent shared memory for compute- and data-intensive applications, UV systems enable organizations to solve complex problems at greater scale, accelerate Big Data analytics and achieve real-time insight, and reduce overhead by eliminating complexity.

SGI® UV™ 300H and SGI® UV™ 300RL are models of the SGI UV 300 supercomputer designed for SAP HANA and Oracle Database In-memory respectively, that enable enterprises to run in real-time at near limitless scale with mission-critical reliability.

Scale Out Solutions

SGI® ICE™ is the world's fastest distributed memory computing system. Built using industry-standard components, SGI ICE leads the industry in performance per watt for x86 systems and claims four out of the top five most power-efficient supercomputers.

SGI® Rackable® servers feature value-driven density and performance with complete factory-integration. SGI® InfiniteData™ Cluster offers breakthrough density with extreme scale-out performance for Big Data analytical workloads such as Hadoop.

Easily Managed, Fully Integrated Environment

SGI's comprehensive management and performance software stack is available for all compute solutions. And high performance Lustre storage and Tiered Data Management solutions enable cost-effective scalability that's virtually limitless.
