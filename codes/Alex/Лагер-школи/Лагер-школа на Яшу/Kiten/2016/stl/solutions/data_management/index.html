In a world where the pressures have never been greater to achieve time to discovery and extract valuable insights from ever-larger pools of data, SGI meets the challenges with some of the world's most powerful Compute and Data Analytics systems. Enabling these high-performance environments are industry-leading SGI® Storage Solutions with performance, throughput and capacity that scale virtually endlessly. By intelligently combining leading storage technologies SGI further makes it possible to meet almost any performance and budget objective.

Tiered Data Management

SGI's suite of Tiered Data Management solutions are proven in some of the world's most demanding high performance environments and address the cost and complexity of managing data at scale. It's more than just storage, it's about an end-to-end strategy to help customers manage the full lifecycle of their data from creation and collection through processing, analysis and ongoing retention and compliance. SGI® DMF™ solutions address storage analytics and file migration, data management, and fabric for tiered storage virtualization.

Scalable Lustre Solutions

SGI's Lustre-based family of products includes world-class Lustre software, high-performance storage hardware and the SGI DMF for Lustre storage data management platform which can extend Lustre systems to almost limitless levels of capacity while minimizing total cost of ownership (TCO). The result is a range of Lustre-based storage solutions that provide the HPC community's highest levels of scalability, performance and reliability.

Storage Hardware

SGI® InfiniteStorage™ hardware offering also includes flexible, reliable and cost-effective servers, JBODs, tape libraries, and infrastructure components
