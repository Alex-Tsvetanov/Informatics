SGI Support Centers

For technical assistance, please call or e-mail your local SGI Customer Support Center. Options and availability for services may vary by country.

Service Providers

In countries which do not host an SGI Customer Support Center, technical assistance is available from our authorized service providers.

See list of Service Providers

The email address, support@sgi.com, is provided for customers to submit service requests or inquiries. Requests may be submitted in the following languages: English, Deutsch, Français, and Mandarin. You may also use SGI Customer Portal or contact us by phone with an urgent system problem. Customers in Japan may use the local alias for local language support.
